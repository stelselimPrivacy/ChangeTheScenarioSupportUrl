Change The Scenario

Change The Scenario, Change The Scenario is the great way to share your scenarios! If you don't like a film's scenarios, you could change it with yours. Then, share it with community or create completely different scenario that has not been exist! Plus, the app has a feature which helps to find a group of people to watch a film together. This is amazingly easy to do! Just create an event with specific date & time, and people join your event! Enjoy using Change The Scenario!
